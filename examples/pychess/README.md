# PyChess

## Chess Game Description

Chess is a strategic board game played between two players on an 8x8 grid. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king by placing it under threat of capture with no legal moves.

### Game Components
- 16 pieces per player (1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, 8 pawns)
- 64-square board
- Turn-based movement

### Objectives
1. Checkmate opponent's king
2. Capture all enemy pieces
3. Stalemate (no moves available and king not in check)

### Movement Rules
- Pawns: Forward 1, capture diagonally
- Knights: L-shaped jumps
- Bishops: Diagonal movement
- Rooks: Straight lines
- Queen: Combines rook/bishop movements
- King: One square in any direction

### Setup
Pieces are arranged with pawns on the second and seventh ranks, major pieces on the first and eighth ranks.
